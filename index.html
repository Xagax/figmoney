The Fig Money Difference

Fig Money is a uniquely powerful homebuying app for first-time home buyers. Fig Money makes sure you know your affordability numbers — like really know — and then takes you every step of the way through the homebuying process. Fig Money helps you avoid the homebuying pitfalls and get the best deals. Oh, and it’s 100% free.

Know What You Can Truly Afford

Start the right way, with you. Fig Money helps you determine your monthly housing budget by taking into account the things that matter, like your income and expenses, your other savings goals and financial priorities, and your expected monthly home costs. Fig Money’s smart forecasting lets you understand the trade-offs that you can make to stretch your budget.

Understand Like A Pro

Fig Money helps you understand your key numbers and how they all interact — all at the push of a button. We’re talking about complex numbers like home bid price, interest rates, down payment, and monthly mortgate payments. Fig Money alerts you to the important stuff, so you know what to focus on. Fig Money makes the homebuying process painless and, dare we say it, fun.

Find Your Dream Team

Fig Money connects you to the real estate agent, mortgage agent, insurance agent, and closing agent that’s best for you. All recommendations are vetted by our team, so you can rest easy knowing that your dream team will help you cross the finish line.

Simple to Use

Fig Money gives you the tools to make great decisions. Through a simple-to-use interface and patent-pending technology, Fig Money will help you see the big picture, understand every step, and be well-prepared ahead of time.

Personalized Insights

Fig Money uses your financial information and inputs to give you tailored insights and recommendations -- like a team of experts at your fingertips. No more generic calculators or vague advice.

Comprehensive
