We're on a mission to democratize personal finance.

57% of Americans are financially unwell. That’s over 136 million people.

You are not alone.

The past 25 years has seen a shift of practically all financial risk onto us consumers. It’s up to us to make all the right decisions at all the right times. We’ve got an uphill battle against some serious hazards like bad products, limited information, and human biases, and we don’t have the tools to overcome them. Any slip up and we pay the price – quite literally – and can even be excluded from the traditional financial system (if we were lucky enough to have access in the first place).

What kind of system is that?

But there’s hope. Powerful technologies like cloud computing and machine learning are now available and we believe that we can harness these technologies on behalf of consumers to level the playing field and make sure our interests come first.

We created Fig Money to do just that. We’ve hard wired consumer interests into our DNA and stand on the notion that we can create market power in ourselves to ensure our best interests will be upheld in every transaction and financial decision we make.

How do we do this?

First, we’ve rewritten the playbook on personal finance from a one-size-fits-all model that pretends we all follow the same life stages at the same ages to a behavioral-science-based model that recognizes that the little decisions can be just as daunting and impactful as the big stuff, and we almost never know where to start or the best way to go about things, let alone how to protect ourselves from costly missteps.

Then, we make it simple, fun, and social, and focused on what each of us needs and wants, because we’re not all carbon copies of each other.

Finally, we make it free for everyone to use. Let financial product providers pay for the privilege of having us as their customer, and not the other way around. We’ll use that money to create new ways to advance our best interests and we’ll keep innovating until the personal financial system is in our hands, where it always belonged.

Join us as we rewrite the personal financial system to put our interests first!
